Q. Describe your favorite editor and explain two outstanding reasons why it is in your favor.

A. Vim is my favorite editor with the reasons being that:
	1. Vim is customizable and extensible. You can configure your vimrc file to a setting that you feel is comfortable to work in.
	2. Vim is very efficient when it comes to editing your source codes by using different keyboard commands which makes it very fast to copy,paste,delete,update lines.
